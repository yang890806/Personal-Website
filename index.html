Introduction

I was born on 6 August, 2000, and have been lived in Taoyuan, Taiwan. There are four members in my family, including my parents, one elder sister, and me. I like eating and listening to the music in my spare time. Sometimes, I spend my summer vacation traveling abroad to experience a lot of different cultures. Although I like to travel, I also like to sleep. And my favorite food are eggs, taro, and cheese.
